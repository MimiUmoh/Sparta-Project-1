Instructions

On each level, you will be asked to click on the right answer or move on to the next level. The more correct answers you get, the more points you accumulate. Your points will be displayed under the title of your current level. If you get an answer wrong, you will recieve an alert message and lose points. You cannot go onto the next level until you click on the right answer. Your aim is to get to the Celestial city. If you complete all the levels, You will recive a Crown of Life at the end of the game. Be faithful until the end. CLICK ON THE IMAGE TO BEGIN.
